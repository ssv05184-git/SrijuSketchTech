About Sriju SketchTech 📚

Sriju SketchTech is a passion project dedicated to making learning more visual, engaging, and fun. We believe that everyone learns differently, and visual learners deserve resources that match their learning style. Our doodle-style notes combine artistic expression with technical knowledge to create a unique educational experience.
